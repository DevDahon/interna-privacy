Privacy Policy

[EFFECTIVE_DATE]: May 18, 2026 [DEVELOPER]: Dahon

1. Who we are

Interna is an OJT management companion designed for students. It helps users track work logs, attach documentation photos, export reports, and generate AI-assisted narrative reports.

Most day-to-day tracking features can be used offline. Internet access is mainly needed for AI generation, rewarded ads, and related backend protections. Interna is independently developed and maintained by Dahon.

2. What data Interna handles

Profile and settings

Interna stores user-entered profile details such as student name, course, school, company name, supervisor name, OJT date range, and required hours.

Work logs and saved reports

Interna stores work logs, dates, time ranges, task descriptions, quantities, captions, and saved generated reports locally on the device.

Proof photos

Interna can capture or select proof images for documentation. These images are stored and used locally on the device and are not uploaded in the normal app flow.

Technical and device data

Interna and its integrated services may process install identifiers, device trust signals, app version details, Android-specific identifiers, diagnostics, and app interaction data needed for protected features.

3. How data is used

  • To operate the app's OJT logging, reporting, and export features.
  • To support offline-first tracking with local device storage.
  • To generate AI narrative reports based on user-entered profile details and work-log content.
  • To provide rewarded-ad credits and enforce weekly generation limits.
  • To protect the app from automated abuse, reinstall abuse, and suspicious usage patterns.
  • To maintain app stability, diagnostics, and service reliability.

4. Ads, AI, and third-party services

Supabase

Interna uses Supabase Edge Functions and related backend services for AI requests, reward-credit handling, and trust or quota enforcement.

OpenRouter and Google Gemini

When AI narrative generation is used, prompt content may include user-entered profile details and work-log data so a narrative can be generated. These requests may be processed by OpenRouter or Google Gemini through the app's backend flow.

Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

Interna uses AdMob for rewarded ads. Depending on SDK behavior and Google's systems, this may involve diagnostics, app interaction data, device or other identifiers, and conservative approximate-location handling derived from network or SDK context. Interna does not provide a user-facing precise-location feature.

5. Device trust and anti-abuse protections

Interna uses technical signals to reduce abuse of AI generation and rewarded credits. On supported Android builds, this may include Play Integrity verification, device-trust data, install identifiers, Android-specific identifiers, and request-specific trust metadata.

These protections are used to detect suspicious reinstall patterns, repeated abuse attempts, and invalid rewarded-credit claims. This system does not require account creation or login.

6. How long data is retained

  • Settings, logs, photos, and saved reports remain on-device until the user deletes them, uninstalls the app, clears app data, or replaces them through restore operations.
  • Backend reward, usage, and trust records may be retained for quota management, fraud prevention, diagnostics, and operational review.
  • AI request content may be processed by backend and AI service providers according to their operational and legal retention practices.

7. Your controls and limitations

Interna does not offer an account-based self-service deletion portal. Users can manage most local app data directly on their device by editing or deleting logs, removing local content, restoring backups, or uninstalling the app.

Users may also contact the privacy email listed below for privacy-related questions about the current app behavior.

8. Security practices

Interna uses encrypted network connections for supported backend, AI, and ad-related requests. As with any digital service, no system can guarantee absolute security, but reasonable technical measures are used to protect data in transit and limit abuse.

9. Children and educational use

Interna is intended for students and educational or OJT-related use. It is not designed as a general children's app for young children. Institutions and users should review this policy before using the app in formal academic settings.
